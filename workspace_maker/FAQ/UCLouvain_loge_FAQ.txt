When do I have to apply for a UCL accommodation  ?
<div><p><strong>As from March</strong> (for the forthcoming academic year) <strong>until </strong>:</p><p><strong>July 31</strong> for a 1st term Exchange&nbsp; ;&nbsp; <strong>November 30</strong> for a 2nd term Exchange<a href="https://uclouvain.be/en/study/accomodation/on-line-accomodation-request-0-1-2.html"> </a></p><p>Please apply online&nbsp; (as from March 5th, 2018)</p><p><strong>UCL accommodation is limited</strong>&nbsp;and the reservations are made following the principle&nbsp;<strong>‘first-come, first-served’</strong>.</p></div>

I must do a visa application. I need a proof of my accommodation request or that I will have an accommodation in my host country. How can I get this document ?
<p>Only use <strong>this e-mail adress for your requests</strong> : &nbsp; inforlogementlln@uclouvain.be (for accommodation in Louvain-la-Neuve)</p>

I've got no answer yet ? 
<p><strong>Please wait</strong> : any request will receive a <strong>follow-up.</strong></p>

What if I receive a letter "no room available" before arriving in Belgium ?
<div><p style="text-align: justify;">You have the possibility to stay in a temporary dorm - the&nbsp;<strong>Kot Erasmus</strong> (28 rooms in a shared flat exclusively for Exchange Students) - for few days (approx. 3 days) in order to take the time to look intensively for an accommodation. Subscribe online:&nbsp;<a href="http://koterasmus.be/">http://koterasmus.be/</a>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p style="text-align: justify;">Try also the <strong>other channels of housing</strong> (menu on the right). See also the question 6. "no more UCL accommodation available"</p></div>

What if I have no room upon my arrival ?
<div><p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>You may still get a chance to have a UCL accommodation during the week before the Welcome Day : </strong></p><p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>go to the UCL Accommodation Office as soon as possible AND every day.</strong></p><p style="text-align: justify;">Some&nbsp;UCL accommodation are released early&nbsp;in the semester. There are several reasons for this :&nbsp; such as students cancelling their arrival or finding another place to live or missing exams.&nbsp;</p><p style="text-align: justify;">Try <strong>other channels of housing</strong> included <strong>the </strong><strong>private owners </strong>: <a href="https://cdn.uclouvain.be/groups/cms-editors-loge/fichiers-proprios/CHAMBRES_24.pdf">click here for rooms</a> /&nbsp; <a href="https://cdn.uclouvain.be/groups/cms-editors-loge/fichiers-proprios/STUDIOS_26.pdf">flats </a>/ <a href="https://cdn.uclouvain.be/groups/cms-editors-loge/fichiers-proprios/APPARTEMENTS_25.pdf">appartments</a> (<u>Last updated October 20, 2017)</u></p></div>

Who can help me upon my arrival ?
<div><p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>The student club "Erasmus Student Network" - ESN LLN,</strong>&nbsp;can help you when you arrive in Belgium if you ask for it. They will&nbsp;<strong>not make the search for a place to live</strong> <strong>for you</strong>&nbsp;but <strong>they will advise you</strong> on how to find a place. They are on&nbsp;<a href="https://www.facebook.com/esn.louvainlaneuve/?fref=ts">Facebook</a>&nbsp;and have a website (<a href="http://www.esnlln.org/">www.esnlln.org</a>); but you can also send them an email at&nbsp;<a href="mailto:esn.lln@gmail.com?subject=LSM%20student%20request">esn.lln@gmail.com</a>.</p><p style="text-align: justify;">ESN LLN can <strong>rent</strong> you a<strong> bedding kit.</strong></p></div>
