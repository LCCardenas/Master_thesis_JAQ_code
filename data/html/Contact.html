      Who can help me upon my arrival ? The student club "Erasmus Student Network" - ESN LLN, can help you when you arrive in Belgium if you ask for it. They will not make the search for a place to live for you but they will advise you on how to find a place. They are on Facebook and have a website (www.esnlln.org); but you can also send them an email at esn.lln@gmail.com. ESN LLN can rent you a bedding kit.
