    The mandatory LSM International Welcome takes place at the beginning of the term. We give you the necessary information to finalize your courses registration and useful information to start your semester at LSM. We provide information as registration to courses and exams, French lessons, sports,... Exchange students will also be invited to an LSM Welcome drink and to "Connect to your account" workshops were UCLouvain cards will be distributed.
    The LSM International Welcome for the Fall Term will take place around mid-September and the one for the Spring Term will be held around beginning of February. Check the program of your day. This one is sent by email to all exchange students.

    Montesquieu Building See map to see how to get to Auditorium Doyen–Room 31/32 (DOYE31/32)

    you need to bring : For non-EU students: your Visa, passport and the proof of your health insurance coverage For EU students: the proof of your health insurance coverage

  • ESN

    UCLouvain's web page

    The Erasmus Student Network section of Louvain-la-Neuve (ESN LLN) is a voluntary student organisation. Their mission is to help you make the most of your exchange in our university, town and country. They will be pleased to welcome you upon your arrival and help you with your administrative tasks.
    They will also organise parties, visits, trips and other activities during your stay. To keep you updated, join their group Erasmus ESN LLN on Facebook. If you want to become a member of ESN, you can get an ESNcard at their office, which will provide you access to their activities, trips, parties and discounts in every city and every country where ESN exists (39 countries).
    Two important things to know before your arrival :
    They offer a pick-up service at the train station of Louvain-la-Neuve and a buddy system. If you would like to be welcomed at the station when you arrive and/or you would like to have a local student as buddy who would help you during your stay, you can fill in the following form as soon as possible and at least 7 days before your arrival: Buddy form
    Your student room has most probably no beddings, no dishes, etc. No worries, ESN LLN has its own second-hand shop where you can buy useful items (at their office). You also have the possibility to rent a bedding kit. For more information or questions, do not hesitate to contact them by Facebook or at esn.lln@gmail.com.
    They will also be present at the UCLouvain International Welcome but you can already get more information on the ESN website.
