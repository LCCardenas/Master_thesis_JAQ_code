# JAQ pairs intents-answers -- Legend
Intent, example of question and answer for 'simple' questions is done as follows:
```markdown
#### Intent
**Example of question**
*Note*

Answer
```

For 'complicated' questions:
```markdown
#### Intent
**Example of question**
*Note*

- *Values of relevant variables*
  *Note*

  Answer

- *Values of relevant variables*
  *Note*

  Answer

- *Values of relevant variables*
  *Cf. ####intent*
```
